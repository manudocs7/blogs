  General trade news

No generic updates.
 No wasted attention.

Yet adoption is shockingly low among MSMEs.
Why?

They don’t know how to claim benefits


They don’t know which markets become attractive


ManuDocs ensures:

Reduce dependency on limited markets


Strengthen India’s trade diversification
