 ## How ManuDocs Is Fixing the Trucking & Logistics Backbone of Indian Trade
From Empty Trucks and Chaos to Coordinated, Transparent Movement

<img src="https://i.postimg.cc/0y5f88Yk/image.png" alt="image" />

After identifying buyers, suppliers, and market intelligence, exporters and importers hit the next hard wall in global trade:
Trucking and logistics execution is fragmented, opaque, inefficient, and wasteful.
Today:
25–30% trucks run empty


Loaded trucks wait 1–3 days for the next job


Exporters pay higher costs


Truckers lose income


Ports, CFSs, and warehouses struggle with unpredictability


ManuDocs solves this not by creating another transport marketplace, but by orchestrating trucking directly into the trade execution flow — using government digital rails.

 The Core Idea: Integrate Trucks Into Trade, Not Outside It
The root cause of inefficiency is simple:
Trucks operate outside trade systems


Trade systems operate without logistics planning


ManuDocs brings them together.

## 5. Solving the Empty Return Problem (Export → Import Loop)

<img src="https://i.postimg.cc/6pmqzwpN/image.png" alt="image" />

This is where the biggest inefficiency gets fixed.


What happens today
Truck unloads export cargo at port


No visibility of return load


Truck returns empty or waits days


What happens on ManuDocs
Importers list upcoming container pickups (ICD / port → factory)


Trucks heading to ports see return-load opportunities


After unloading export cargo, truckers can:


Choose an import load


Load immediately


Avoid empty return


 This happens by choice, not force.
 Truckers decide which job to accept.
Result:
Empty miles reduce


Trucker income increases


Exporter & importer logistics cost drops
